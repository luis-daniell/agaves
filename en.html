THE IDENTITY OF MEZCALL

The maguey represents history, culture, and the diversity of mexico, each finished bottle has on its own the sweat of the field`s hand, the weight of the grinding `s tahona, the energy that the astros has given to the plant for 7, 10, 15, or even 25 years.

In each sip you will breathe the love of the families that produced it, the devotion of the mezcalero master.

In oaxaca 25 thousand families are dedicated to the production of mezcal in its traditonal form.

It is an honor for us in oaxaca, to be able to share with the world the process that has been heritage of our elders and is our duty to preserve it, but also to give it a quantitative value that it deserves, and not only by speech.
